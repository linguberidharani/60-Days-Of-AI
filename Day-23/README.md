# 🚀 Day 23 – Build Your Customer & MVP Blueprint

## 📌 Objective

Today I learned how to transform a startup idea into a customer-focused product strategy using Claude AI. Instead of jumping directly into development, I created a structured Customer & MVP Blueprint to identify target users, prioritize essential features, validate assumptions, and design a focused Minimum Viable Product (MVP).

---

## 🧠 What I Learned

- Customer discovery and validation
- Defining an Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
- Creating a Buyer Persona
- Identifying customer pain points
- Mapping the customer journey
- Prioritizing product features using the MoSCoW framework
- Designing an MVP with clear success metrics
- Planning a structured 30-day product roadmap
