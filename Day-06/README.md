# Day 6 - AI Resume Optimizer

## Objective

Learn how AI can improve resume quality, ATS compatibility, and recruiter readability.

---

## Prompt Used

You are an expert ATS Resume Optimizer and Technical Recruiter.

Analyze my resume and provide:

1. ATS Score (out of 100)
2. Strengths of the resume
3. Weaknesses and improvement areas
4. Missing keywords for AI, Cybersecurity, Data Analytics, and Software Engineering roles
5. Formatting recommendations
6. Content recommendations
7. Project recommendations
8. Skills recommendations

Then generate an optimized one-page ATS-friendly resume that:

* Keeps all information truthful
* Improves readability
* Improves recruiter appeal
* Improves ATS compatibility
* Highlights projects, technical skills, and achievements
* Uses strong action verbs
* Is suitable for internships and entry-level technical roles

Finally provide:

* Estimated ATS score after optimization
* Top improvements made

---

## Original Resume Analysis

I uploaded my resume and requested an ATS analysis.

### Strengths Identified

* Interest in AI and Cybersecurity

### Areas for Improvement

* More ATS keywords
* Stronger action verbs

---

## Key Learnings

1. ATS systems rely heavily on relevant keywords.
2. Projects should demonstrate impact, not just tasks.
3. Quantifiable achievements improve recruiter interest.
4. Resume structure affects ATS parsing.
5. AI can significantly improve resume presentation while keeping information truthful.

---

## Biggest Takeaway

A resume should be optimized for both ATS systems and human recruiters. AI can help identify weaknesses, improve keyword coverage, and create a more professional presentation of existing skills and experiences.
