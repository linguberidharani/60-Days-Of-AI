Why this system prompt

The advisor is scoped tightly to handmade-craft growth strategy — not general business consulting — so it can give specific, grounded advice instead of generic startup platitudes. It's instructed to ask before assuming, never invent numbers or facts, and always land on a concrete 3–5 step action plan rather than open-ended brainstorming.

Why this interface

Growth planning for a craft business is personal and visual, so the layout pairs a conversational advisor with a living "session snapshot" — a growth-score spool gauge, KPI cards, and a stitched action-plan list — so progress feels tangible rather than buried in chat text.

How to extend this

  • Memory: persist form + chat history across sessions (localStorage or a backend) so returning makers don't re-enter context.
  • Tools: add function calling for live Etsy fee lookups or shipping-cost calculators.
  • File uploads / RAG: parse full sales-history spreadsheets and retrieve relevant rows into context.
  • Multi-agent: split into a pricing-specialist and a marketing-specialist sub-agent, orchestrated by this advisor.
Craft Business Growth Advisor — a focused planning session, not a substitute for legal, tax, or financial advice.
