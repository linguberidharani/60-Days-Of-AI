# 🚀 Day 25 – Build an AI Shark Tank Simulator

## 📌 Objective

Today I built an interactive AI Shark Tank Simulator using Claude AI. The application allows users to pitch startup ideas, receive feedback from AI judges, evaluate business potential, and generate investment decisions through an engaging simulation.

---

## 🧠 What I Learned

- AI-powered startup evaluation
- Interactive product design
- Multi-agent AI simulations
- Investment decision frameworks
- Business scoring methodologies
- Front-end application generation using AI
- Modern HTML, CSS, and JavaScript development

---

## 💼 Startup Idea Used

**Punch & Thread Co.**

A handmade punch needle art business offering personalized home décor, gifts, tote bags, coasters, wall art, and custom handmade products.

---

## 🦈 AI Judges

The simulator included four AI investors:

- 🦈 Venture Capitalist
- 🦈 Startup Founder
- 🦈 Customer Representative
- 🦈 Angel Investor

Each judge evaluated the startup from a different perspective and provided valuable feedback.

---

## 📊 Evaluation Criteria

The startup was assessed on:

- Market Potential
- Innovation
- Business Model
- Execution
- Investment Worthiness

---

## 💰 Final Investment Decision

The simulator generated:

- Investment Recommendation
- Suggested Valuation
- Funding Amount
- Business Strengths
- Key Risks
- Improvement Suggestions

---

## 💡 Key Learning

AI can simulate realistic investor conversations, helping founders improve their startup ideas before approaching real investors.
