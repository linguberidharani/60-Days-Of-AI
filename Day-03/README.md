# Day 3 - Role-Based Prompting

## Objective

Learn how assigning different roles to AI changes the style and quality of responses.

## What is Role-Based Prompting?

Role-Based Prompting is a technique where you tell the AI to act as a specific expert before asking a question.

Examples:

* Founder
* Developer
* Product Manager
* HR Manager
* Marketer

This helps the AI respond from that expert's perspective instead of giving a general answer.

## Question Used

How can I prepare for a career in AI and Cybersecurity while studying B.Tech?

## Without Role Prompt

Prompt:
How can I prepare for a career in AI and Cybersecurity while studying B.Tech?

Observation:
The response was helpful but generic and broad.

## Founder Persona

Prompt:
You are a successful AI startup founder.

How can I prepare for a career in AI and Cybersecurity while studying B.Tech?

Observation:
The response focused on entrepreneurship, networking, product building, and personal branding.

## Developer Persona

Prompt:
You are a senior AI software engineer with 15 years of experience.

How can I prepare for a career in AI and Cybersecurity while studying B.Tech?

Observation:
The response focused on technical skills, coding practice, projects, GitHub, and engineering fundamentals.

## Key Learnings

1. Different personas provide different perspectives.
2. Role-Based Prompting improves response relevance.
3. AI becomes more specialized when given a role.
4. Better prompts lead to better decisions.

## Biggest Takeaway

Role-Based Prompting transforms AI from a general assistant into a domain expert.
