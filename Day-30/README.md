# 🚚 Day 30 - Supply Chain Builder
### 60 Days Of AI Challenge

An interactive **Supply Chain Optimization Simulator** that helps beginners understand how real-world supply chains are designed, optimized, and managed through strategic decisions.

This project transforms complex operations concepts into an engaging business simulation where users act as supply chain leaders and make decisions that impact cost, delivery speed, risk, customer satisfaction, and sustainability.

---

## 🌟 Project Overview

Supply chains connect suppliers, factories, warehouses, transportation networks, and customers.

In this simulator, users build their own supply chain strategy by making important operational decisions and observing their real-time business impact.

The goal is to create a balanced supply chain that reduces cost, improves efficiency, minimizes risk, and keeps customers satisfied.

---

## 🎯 Objective

Build and optimize a supply chain by choosing:

- Supplier strategy
- Factory location
- Warehouse approach
- Transportation method
- Inventory strategy

Every decision creates trade-offs, just like real business environments.

---

# ✨ Features

## 🏭 Random Company Generation

Each simulation generates a unique company profile:

- Industry type
- Products
- Countries served
- Demand level
- Business requirements

Every playthrough provides a different challenge.

---

## 🔗 Supply Chain Building Decisions

Users make five major decisions:

### 1. Supplier Strategy

Choose between:

- Single Supplier
- Multiple Suppliers

Learn how supplier choices affect:

- Cost
- Reliability
- Supply chain risk

---

### 2. Factory Location

Select production locations based on:

- Manufacturing cost
- Market proximity
- Delivery speed

---

### 3. Warehouse Strategy

Choose:

- Centralized warehouse
- Regional warehouses

Understand the balance between:

- Storage cost
- Delivery efficiency
- Customer response time

---

### 4. Transportation Method

Compare transportation options:

- 🚚 Road
- 🚆 Rail
- 🚢 Sea
- ✈️ Air

Each option impacts:

- Speed
- Cost
- Sustainability

---

### 5. Inventory Strategy

Select inventory levels:

- Low Inventory
- Balanced Inventory
- High Inventory

Understand the relationship between:

- Storage cost
- Product availability
- Customer satisfaction
