# 🚀 Day 22 – Validate Your Startup Idea Like a VC

## 📌 Objective

Today I explored how AI can help validate startup ideas using a structured venture capital (VC) approach. Instead of relying on assumptions, I used Claude AI to analyze a startup idea, evaluate market opportunities, identify risks, and generate an actionable business validation report.

---

## 💡 Startup Idea

**Punch Needle Art Studio**

A small business focused on creating handcrafted punch needle art products such as wall décor, personalized gifts, home accessories, and custom handmade creations. The goal is to build a sustainable business that supports financial independence while promoting creative craftsmanship.
