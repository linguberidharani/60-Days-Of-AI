# 🚢 Day 29 — Operation Lifeline: Supply Chain Crisis Simulator

> Part of my **60 Days of AI Challenge**

An interactive AI-powered business crisis simulation where users take the role of a decision-maker managing a company's supply chain disruption.

The simulation places users inside a high-pressure executive environment where every decision impacts business metrics like cost efficiency, inventory health, delivery speed, customer satisfaction, and risk management.

---

## 🎯 Project Overview

**Operation Lifeline** is a strategic crisis management simulator designed to replicate real-world enterprise decision-making.

Users guide **Northgate Global**, a semiconductor manufacturing company, through a major shipping delay crisis.

From the first crisis alert to the final CEO boardroom review, users must balance:

- Business continuity
- Customer expectations
- Supplier relationships
- Cost control
- Long-term resilience

Every choice creates measurable consequences.
