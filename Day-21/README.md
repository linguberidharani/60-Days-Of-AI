# 🚀 Day 21 – Build a Digital Privacy Intelligence Dashboard

## 📌 Objective

Today I built an interactive **Digital Privacy Intelligence Dashboard** using Claude AI. The dashboard analyzes a sample digital footprint dataset, estimates privacy exposure, identifies tracking risks, and presents the results through an interactive cybersecurity-style dashboard.

---

## 🧠 What I Learned

- Digital privacy and online footprint analysis
- Estimating privacy exposure using AI
- Understanding ecosystem concentration across digital platforms
- Risk assessment and data collection visualization
- Building interactive dashboards with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
- Presenting cybersecurity insights through modern UI design

---

## 🛡️ Dashboard Features

- 📊 Digital Footprint Score
- 🔒 Privacy Score
- 🌐 Exposure Heatmap
- 🏢 Company Exposure Ranking
- 📋 Data Collection Matrix
- ⚠️ Risk Radar
- 👤 Digital Twin Profile
- 💎 Most Valuable Data Assets
- 🛠️ Privacy Improvement Simulator
- 📈 Final Privacy Verdict

## 💡 Key Learning

This project demonstrated how AI can transform structured digital activity data into meaningful privacy insights. Instead of simply displaying information, AI can estimate privacy exposure, highlight potential tracking risks, and recommend actionable improvements while clearly separating facts from estimates.

## 🚀 Outcome

Successfully built a modern Digital Privacy Intelligence Dashboard capable of visualizing digital footprints, estimating privacy exposure, identifying tracking risks, and generating personalized privacy improvement recommendations.
