Quarterly growth strategy meeting

Transcript mode · Speakers: CEO, Head of Sales, CFO, VP Marketing, Product Director, CTO, Customer Success Lead

Summary
Q2 revenue grew 12%, missing the 18% annual target, mainly due to enterprise deals slipping (9 of 14 expected contracts closed) from procurement delays and security review requests. Marketing reported a 34% rise in website traffic but lower-than-expected conversion, prompting a planned redesign that engineering capacity may not support without contractors. Customer retention is strong at 94%, but support tickets have risen due to reporting performance and dashboard loading issues, which the CEO flagged as a priority. Decisions on hiring, the annual conference budget, and any public announcement of the August analytics dashboard were all postponed pending further information.
