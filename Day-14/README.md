# Day 14 – AI Job Red Flag Detector

## Objective

Learn how AI can evaluate job opportunities by identifying hidden risks, misleading job descriptions, and potential workplace concerns before applying.

---

## Activities Completed

* Collected a Job Description (JD)
* Gathered company information
* Used Claude to analyze the opportunity
* Reviewed the overall risk score
* Identified red flags and positive signals
* Evaluated hiring, culture, and remote-work indicators
* Generated smart interview questions
* Documented key insights and learnings

---

## Analysis Performed

### Risk Detection

* Unrealistic job requirements
* Excessive skill expectations
* Contradictory responsibilities

### Workplace Culture

* Burnout indicators
* Fast-paced work environment
* Work-life balance considerations
* Team culture signals

### Hiring Process

* Salary transparency
* Job description clarity
* Required qualifications
* Recruitment practices

### Company Evaluation

* Reputation indicators
* Stability signals
* Growth opportunities
* Overall hiring confidence

---

## Key Learnings

* AI can identify hidden warning signs in job descriptions.
* A job application is a two-way evaluation process.
* Salary transparency and clear responsibilities improve job quality.
* Asking thoughtful interview questions helps validate concerns.
* Evaluating company culture is just as important as evaluating the role.

---

## Outcome

Built an AI-powered Job Red Flag Detection workflow that helps evaluate opportunities before investing time in the application process.
