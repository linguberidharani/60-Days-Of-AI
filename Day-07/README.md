# Day 7 - Claude Model Selection & Reasoning Effort

## Objective
Learn how to choose the right Claude model and reasoning level for different tasks.

## Key Learnings

### Claude Models

#### Haiku
- Fastest model
- Best for quick lookups
- Ideal for flashcards and simple questions

#### Sonnet
- Balanced model
- Best for daily usage
- Great for coding, learning, and content creation

#### Opus
- Deepest reasoning
- Best for research and complex planning
- Useful for interview preparation and AI research

## Reasoning Effort Levels

- Low → Quick answers
- Standard → Daily tasks
- High → Complex analysis
- Max → Deep research and critical decisions

## My Personalized Strategy

Primary Model: Sonnet

Use Cases:
- LinkedIn content
- Cybersecurity learning
- Portfolio projects
- AI concepts

Use Opus for:
- Research papers
- Interview preparation
- Advanced project planning

Use Haiku for:
- Quick definitions
- German vocabulary
- Flashcards

## Biggest Insight

I learned that selecting the right model is often more important than writing a perfect prompt. Different models are optimized for different levels of reasoning and complexity.
